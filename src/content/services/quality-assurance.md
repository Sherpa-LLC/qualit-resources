## Overview

Software quality is not optional — it is the foundation of user trust and business success. At Qual IT Resources, we specialize in connecting organizations with QA professionals who bring the technical depth, methodological rigor, and practical experience needed to ensure your software meets the highest standards.

Whether you need a single automation engineer to bolster your team or an entire QA department to support a major release, we deliver talent that makes a measurable difference in your software quality.

## What We Offer

- **Manual Testing Specialists** — Experienced testers who excel at exploratory testing, regression testing, user acceptance testing, and test case design. They catch what automation misses.
- **Automation Engineers** — Professionals who design, build, and maintain robust test automation frameworks that accelerate your release cycles and catch regressions early.
- **Performance & Load Testing** — Specialists who ensure your applications perform under real-world conditions using tools like JMeter, Gatling, and LoadRunner.
- **QA Leadership** — QA managers and leads who can establish testing strategies, build teams, define processes, and drive quality culture across your organization.
- **SDET Specialists** — Software Development Engineers in Test who bridge the gap between development and QA, embedding quality into the codebase itself.

## Our QA Staffing Process

1. **Requirements Analysis** — We work with you to understand your technology stack, testing methodology, team dynamics, and specific quality challenges.
2. **Candidate Matching** — We draw from our network of pre-vetted QA professionals to identify candidates whose skills and experience align with your needs.
3. **Technical Evaluation** — Every QA candidate undergoes hands-on technical assessment covering their specific domain — whether that is automation scripting, test design, or performance testing.
4. **Presentation & Interview** — We present a shortlist of top candidates with detailed profiles covering their technical skills, project experience, and tool proficiency.
5. **Placement & Follow-Up** — We ensure a smooth onboarding and check in regularly to confirm the placement is delivering the quality impact you expected.
