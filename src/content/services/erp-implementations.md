## Overview

ERP implementations are among the most complex and high-stakes projects an organization undertakes. The difference between a successful deployment and a costly failure often comes down to the people on the project. At Qual IT Resources, we specialize in placing ERP professionals who have been through the trenches — professionals who bring not just technical knowledge but practical implementation experience that keeps projects on track.

We staff across the full ERP project lifecycle, from initial planning and requirements gathering through configuration, testing, data migration, go-live, and post-implementation support.

## Roles We Place

- **ERP Project Managers** — Seasoned project leaders who manage scope, timeline, budget, and stakeholder expectations across complex, multi-module implementations.
- **Functional Consultants** — Subject matter experts in specific ERP modules (Finance, HR, Supply Chain, Manufacturing, etc.) who translate business requirements into system configurations.
- **Technical Developers** — ABAP, PL/SQL, X++, and other ERP-specific developers who build custom reports, interfaces, conversions, enhancements, and forms (RICEF/WRICEF).
- **Data Migration Specialists** — Professionals who plan and execute the critical data migration from legacy systems to your new ERP, ensuring data integrity and completeness.
- **Change Management Professionals** — Specialists who manage the human side of ERP implementation, driving user adoption through communication, training, and organizational readiness.
- **Integration Architects** — Experts who design and build the integration layer connecting your ERP with other enterprise systems, third-party applications, and data sources.

## Why ERP Staffing Matters

ERP projects fail at alarmingly high rates — industry estimates suggest that 50-75% of ERP implementations experience significant challenges. The most common causes are not technical. They are people-related: insufficient expertise, poor change management, lack of project leadership, and misalignment between business requirements and technical execution.

By placing experienced, battle-tested ERP professionals on your project, we help you avoid these pitfalls and deliver a successful implementation that realizes the business value your organization is investing in.

## Our Process

1. **Project Assessment** — We review your implementation plan, technology platform, module scope, and timeline to understand exactly what talent you need and when.
2. **Targeted Search** — We activate our network of ERP professionals, targeting candidates with specific platform, module, and industry experience that matches your project.
3. **Deep Vetting** — ERP candidates undergo detailed evaluation including implementation history review, platform certification verification, and reference checks from previous project stakeholders.
4. **Team Integration** — We ensure our placements integrate smoothly with your internal team and systems integrator, aligning on methodology, tools, and communication protocols.
5. **Ongoing Support** — We maintain close communication throughout the engagement to address any concerns and ensure the placement is meeting project expectations.
