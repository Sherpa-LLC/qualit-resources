## Overview

Data is the lifeblood of modern business. The organizations that harness it effectively outperform those that do not — and the difference comes down to people. At Qual IT Resources, we specialize in placing data professionals who do not just manage databases but who transform raw data into strategic advantage.

From database administrators who keep your systems running to data scientists who uncover insights that drive revenue, we connect you with the data talent that moves your business forward.

## Roles We Place

- **Database Administrators** — Expert DBAs for SQL Server, Oracle, PostgreSQL, MySQL, and MongoDB who ensure your data infrastructure is reliable, performant, and secure.
- **Data Architects** — Professionals who design scalable data ecosystems, define data models, and establish the architectural foundations for your analytics and reporting capabilities.
- **Data Engineers** — Builders who construct and maintain the data pipelines, ETL processes, and integration layers that feed your analytics and business intelligence platforms.
- **Data Scientists** — Analysts who apply statistical methods, machine learning, and advanced analytics to extract actionable insights from your data.
- **Business Intelligence Analysts** — Professionals who translate data into dashboards, reports, and visualizations that inform business decisions.
- **Machine Learning Engineers** — Specialists who build, deploy, and maintain ML models in production environments, turning data science prototypes into operational systems.

## Technologies & Platforms

Our data professionals bring expertise across the modern data ecosystem:

- **Databases** — SQL Server, Oracle, PostgreSQL, MySQL, MongoDB, Cassandra, DynamoDB
- **Cloud Data Platforms** — AWS Redshift, Azure Synapse, Google BigQuery, Snowflake, Databricks
- **Data Engineering** — Apache Spark, Kafka, Airflow, dbt, Informatica, Talend
- **BI & Visualization** — Tableau, Power BI, Looker, Qlik, SSRS
- **Languages** — SQL, Python, R, Scala
- **ML/AI Frameworks** — TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, MLflow

## Our Approach

1. **Technical Requirements Deep Dive** — We go beyond job descriptions to understand your data architecture, technology stack, and the specific challenges your team faces.
2. **Specialized Recruiting** — Our data-focused recruiters know the difference between a data engineer and a data analyst. We target candidates with the exact skills your role requires.
3. **Hands-On Assessment** — Data candidates undergo practical evaluation including SQL proficiency testing, data modeling exercises, and scenario-based problem solving.
4. **Cultural Alignment** — Data professionals often work across teams. We evaluate communication skills, collaboration style, and business acumen alongside technical ability.
5. **Long-Term Partnership** — We follow up after placement to ensure your new data professional is delivering value and integrating successfully with your team.
