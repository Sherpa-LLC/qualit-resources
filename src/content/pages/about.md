## Our Story

Since our founding in 2017, Qual IT Resources has been driven by a deep passion for staffing and recruiting. Based in Blue Ash, Ohio, we have grown from a focused IT staffing firm into a comprehensive STEM recruiting and consulting partner trusted by enterprise organizations across the Cincinnati region and beyond.

What started as a commitment to bridging the gap between top technical talent and the companies that need them has evolved into a full-service staffing operation. Our team brings decades of combined experience across the IT and STEM landscape, giving us the insight and connections to deliver results that matter.

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to find the top IT and STEM talent that drives organizational success. We believe that the right people in the right roles can transform a business. That belief fuels everything we do — from our rigorous vetting process to our personalized approach to every client engagement.

We are committed to developing enduring partnerships with our clients, our consultants, and our internal professionals. We measure our success not just by placements made, but by the lasting value those placements create for everyone involved.

## Our Expertise

Our team's extensive professional network and sector-specific knowledge set us apart. We conduct thorough vetting of every applicant, assessing technical abilities, qualifications, and organizational alignment. This meticulous approach ensures that the candidates we present are not just technically qualified — they are the right cultural fit for your team.

We specialize in placing professionals across a wide range of IT and STEM disciplines:

- **Software Development** — Web, mobile, and enterprise application developers
- **Data & Analytics** — Database administrators, data architects, and data scientists
- **Quality Assurance** — Automation engineers and manual testing specialists
- **Infrastructure & Cloud** — Cloud architects, DevOps engineers, and systems administrators
- **Program Management** — Project managers, business analysts, and Scrum Masters
- **ERP & Enterprise Systems** — SAP, Oracle, and enterprise implementation specialists

## Unparalleled Service

We recognize that every project is unique and every organization has its own culture, challenges, and goals. That is why we take the time to listen — really listen — to what our clients need. Our approach involves attentive consultation, customized staffing solutions, and a streamlined hiring process that respects your time and budget.
